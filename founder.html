Alan Vines

Founder & CEO · ShelfCat Solutions LLC

Mission

ShelfCat was built on a simple idea: the physical world should be measurable, enforceable, and trustworthy — automatically.

From retail shelves to national energy systems, the world suffers from a massive enforcement gap. ShelfCat closes that gap with <100ms autonomous AI enforcement, transforming compliance from a human-dependent process into a solved problem.

Background

Alan is a systems architect and AI engineer specializing in multimodal inference, Rust-based edge computing, and Azure-aligned cloud orchestration. He designed the ShelfCat enforcement pipeline — a patented, six-layer architecture capable of real-time physical-layer enforcement across retail, commercial buildings, industrial facilities, and federal sites.

Technical Leadership

Alan led the development of the full ShelfCat ecosystem:

  • Rust enforcement engine with TPM 2.0 proof-of-action
  • YOLOv8 + CLIP + FAISS detection pipeline
  • Azure IoT Hub + Stream Analytics telemetry stack
  • Federated learning with ε=8 differential privacy
  • iOS scanner app with CoreML and real-time feedback
  • Next.js dashboard for multi-sector compliance visibility

DOE Alignment

Under Alan's leadership, ShelfCat advanced into DOE Phase II readiness with validated Phase I results:

  • 73% false-positive reduction
  • 89% latency reduction
  • 4.1× energy waste recall improvement
  • 10,000× enforcement cycle acceleration

ShelfCat's architecture directly fulfills the DOE Genesis Mission mandate for AI-driven energy transformation and open-science dissemination.

Vision

Alan's long-term vision is a universal trusted data layer — a world where every physical system is continuously measured, verified, and enforced autonomously. ShelfCat is the first step toward that future.
