Why Federated Learning?

Energy enforcement varies across buildings, climates, equipment, and occupancy patterns. ShelfCat learns from all of them — without ever centralizing raw sensor data.

  • Zero raw video, audio, LiDAR, or thermal data leaves the facility
  • Only gradient updates are shared
  • Differential privacy ensures individual sites cannot be reconstructed
  • OTA model updates continuously improve enforcement accuracy

Federated Learning Pipeline

The full six-step process that powers ShelfCat's network effect.

  • 1 — Local Learning Edge nodes compute gradients from enforcement events.
  • 2 — Differential Privacy ε = 8, δ = 10⁻⁵ noise injection ensures privacy guarantees.
  • 3 — Secure Aggregation Central server aggregates gradients using FedAvg.
  • 4 — Model Improvement Updated weights outperform individual-site models.
  • 5 — OTA Distribution Improved models pushed back to all nodes with zero downtime.
  • 6 — National Intelligence NEVPD: National Energy Violation Pattern Database published to DOE ASC.
